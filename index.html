About us

We are an US association fighting against racism.

We need volunteers to help us to communicate, to teach and help coloured peoples who can't pay for their studies, or who have difficulties with the officials papers and declarations.

We need your support to build shelters, help colored peoples with vouchers, or to make a partnership to compensate volunteers and help colored with their studies.
